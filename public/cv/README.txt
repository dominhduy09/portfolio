This folder is for storing your CV/Resume file.

To add your CV:
1. Place your CV file (PDF recommended) in this folder
2. Name it something like: Minh_Duy_Do_Resume.pdf

The "Download CV" button in your portfolio will link to:
/cv/Minh_Duy_Do_Resume.pdf

Your CV will be downloadable from the Hero section when users click "Download CV".
